About Chad

Educator, Coach, and Practitioner — Not a Pundit

I work in a school every day. I teach students digital media, game design, broadcast production, and AI literacy. I coach basketball. I lead a department navigating the real challenges of technology integration, curriculum design, and building culture with young people.

Everything I write and share comes from that ground-level experience — not from a conference talk or a consultancy report. If it doesn't work in an actual classroom or gym, I'm not interested in selling it.

I write here every two weeks. You can find my professional background on LinkedIn. If you want to talk, email me.
